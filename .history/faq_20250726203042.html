Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Godfrey Okoye University

How do I apply for admission?

Visit the Admissions page, review the requirements, and follow the instructions to complete your application online.

What programmes are offered at GOUNI?

GOUNI offers a wide range of accredited undergraduate and postgraduate programmes in Sciences, Law, Management, Arts, Social Sciences, Education, and Engineering. See the Courses page for details.

Does GOUNI offer scholarships?

Yes, the university offers scholarships and grants to outstanding and deserving students. Visit the News page for updates on scholarship opportunities.

Is accommodation available on campus?

Yes, GOUNI provides comfortable hostel accommodation for students. Early application is advised as spaces are limited.

How can I contact the university?

You can reach us via email at info@gouni.edu.ng or call 09134273215, 09121963049.
