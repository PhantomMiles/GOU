About Godfrey Okoye University

Excellence in Education, Character, and Service

Our History

Godfrey Okoye University (GOUNI), founded in 2009 by the Catholic Diocese of Enugu, is a leading private university in Nigeria. Established to provide quality education rooted in Catholic values, GOUNI has grown into a vibrant academic community, offering accredited undergraduate and postgraduate programs across diverse disciplines. The university is committed to nurturing leaders who are innovative, ethical, and dedicated to the service of humanity.

Our Vision

To be a world-class Catholic university, distinguished for academic excellence, character formation, and commitment to the unity of knowledge and society. We aspire to nurture graduates who are innovative, ethical, and equipped to make a positive impact in Nigeria and beyond.

Our Mission

Godfrey Okoye University is dedicated to providing holistic education rooted in Catholic values, fostering intellectual growth, moral integrity, and social responsibility. Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and values for lifelong learning, leadership, and service to humanity.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in teaching, research, and service.
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty, transparency, and strong moral principles in all our actions.
  • Respect: We foster a culture of respect for diversity, human dignity, and the environment.
  • Innovation: We encourage creativity and embrace new ideas for continuous improvement.
  • Community: We are committed to building a supportive and inclusive academic community.

Why Choose GOUNI?

  • Accredited programs and experienced faculty
  • Modern facilities and a serene learning environment
  • Strong emphasis on character formation and leadership
  • Vibrant campus life with diverse extracurricular activities
  • Supportive alumni network and career development opportunities
