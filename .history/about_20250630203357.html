About Godfrey Okoye University

Excellence in Education, Character, and Service

Our History

Godfrey Okoye University (GOUNI), founded in 2009 by the Catholic Diocese of Enugu, is a leading private university in Nigeria. Established to provide quality education rooted in Catholic values, GOUNI has grown into a vibrant academic community, offering accredited undergraduate and postgraduate programs across diverse disciplines. The university is committed to nurturing leaders who are innovative, ethical, and dedicated to the service of humanity. Over the years, GOUNI has established itself as a center of academic excellence, innovation, and character formation. The university offers a wide range of programs in Arts, Education, Law, Management, Natural and Applied Sciences, and Social Sciences. With a commitment to holistic education, GOUNI provides students with modern facilities, experienced faculty, and a vibrant campus life. The university fosters partnerships with local and international institutions, promoting research, entrepreneurship, and global exposure for its students. Our graduates are known for their integrity, leadership, and positive impact in various sectors within Nigeria and beyond.

Our Vision

To be a world-class Catholic university, distinguished for academic excellence, character formation, and commitment to the unity of knowledge and society. We aspire to nurture graduates who are innovative, ethical, and equipped to make a positive impact in Nigeria and beyond.

Our Mission

Godfrey Okoye University is dedicated to providing holistic education rooted in Catholic values, fostering intellectual growth, moral integrity, and social responsibility. Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and values for lifelong learning, leadership, and service to humanity.
